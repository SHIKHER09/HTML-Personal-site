Future Doore

The team members of our team are undergraduates with a computer science background. In our college, there is no reliable source to find information about research-related topics. Additionally, we found it hard to find the exact article we needed on any other platform we had access to. Moreover, we learned that many students are unfamiliar with how to start/get into the field of research. To tackle this situation, we created a web application called "Future-Door" which is an exclusive platform made only for people who are interested in research, where it enables the user to help out getting connected with the trainer, particularly of their research field of interest. In this way, research trainees as well as mentors will get an actual path from where they can get connected. Our model also offers a one on one chat system for the mentors and mentees which will get enabled only when both mentor and mentee will get matched. Also, it provides a pathway to all the people to access any research-related information (all the published articles in any research fields, about the author of any published article, and much more) on one platform. Also, this project allows users to connect globally with mentors and companies who hire research trainees all over the world via an open chat system, where they can ask about their queries, engage in open discussions in groups, and learn about new trends in the field of research. We have also offered a feed section in our model where users can share their views regarding any research.
